How CopyCat Works

You click on the "Start" button and one of the colours on the screen will flash and also make a sound. You follow this pattern by clicking the colour that flashed. If you guess correctly, an alert will flash up on the screen saying "Well Done! Next Round". The process will then repeat, with an extra move being added with every round. If you guess incorrectly an alert will pop up on the screen saying "Unlucky...Try Again!". The pattern will then be repeated to remind you of the sequence, and once the pattern is complete you can try follow it again. Once you have reached a count of 20, an alert will pop up on the screen saying "You have completed the game...Congratulations!!!".

Strict Mode - Once you enable Strict Mode, if you get the pattern wrong, there is no second chances. An alert will pop up on the screen saying "Hard Luck...Start Again!".

Best of luck and I hope you enjoy the game!!!
